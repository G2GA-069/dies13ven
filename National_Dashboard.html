Valuation Methodology

Securities (Finanzanlagen) — MKT_FA = 1.29x
Weighted average of 9 auditor-confirmed hidden reserve disclosures from Anhang notes: Paderborn 1.33x (€1,507M delta, 2024), Köln 1.33x (€1,095M, 2024), Fulda HI-BF 1.19x (€88.5M, 2024), Fulda FL-BF/LGT 1.45x (€49.2M, 2024), Fulda Aachener 1.14x (€1.5M, 2024), Würzburg WP-Spezialfonds 1.06x (€4.9M, 2024), Würzburg Immobilienfonds 1.03x (€0.2M, 2024), Aachen Spezialfonds 1.06x (€55.9M, 2024), Bamberg Emeritenanstalt UIN-EMBA 1.31x (€37.7M, 2023). Total observed: €9,658M book → €12,498M market = 1.294x weighted average. Range: 1.03x – 1.45x.

Property (Sachanlagen) — MKT_SA = 2.5x
No diocese discloses property fair value. Derived from: (1) Bamberg valued Grundstücke at Verkehrswert in 2018 opening balance (≈1.0x already); (2) Würzburg/Fulda use Bodenrichtwert for land but churches at €1; (3) Berlin SA €1,054M at historical cost in prime market implies 3-5x; (4) München €1,600M (40% of BS) in Bavarian capital market. 2.5x is conservative national weighted average. Sensitivity: ±0.5x SA multiplier = ±€3.0B on entity-level market value.

Pension Liabilities — IAS 19 = 1.3x HGB
HGB method: Heubeck 2018 G mortality tables, Teilwertverfahren, 7yr/10yr avg Marktzins (1.82-1.96% at Dec 2024), 15yr Restlaufzeit. IAS 19: PBO at AA corporate bond rate (~3.2% Dec 2024). Current rate environment means IAS 19 PBO is only modestly above HGB (1.3x), unlike pre-2022 when spread was wider (1.5-2.0x). 7 Bavarian dioceses show €0 or near-€0 pension provisions because obligations sit in separate Emeritenanstalt entities (Bamberg: €185M, Regensburg: €353M, Passau: €254M confirmed).

Other Liabilities — 5% of BS
From 11 extracted Verbindlichkeiten disclosures: actual sum €528M on BS of €10,607M = 5.0%. Includes trade payables, intercompany to Kirchenstiftungen, Sonderposten amortization, bank debt (minimal — Freiburg: €91K). Largest: Bamberg €87M (€78M intercompany), Osnabrück €54M, Paderborn €138M.

Unextracted Dioceses — 1.5x blended
9 dioceses lack SA/FA breakdown. Blended 1.5x applied (below 1.67x weighted avg of extracted dioceses). These 9 represent €9,462M book value = 28% of total.
