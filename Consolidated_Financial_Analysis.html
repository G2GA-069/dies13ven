Measurement Approach

We use IFRS 10 consolidation logic to define the perimeter (all 7 layers) but measure everything at estimated market value. IFRS fair value (IAS 40, IAS 19) is an accounting construct that serves auditors. For an institutional audience, we go straight to market value. The result: one consolidated MV balance sheet. No HGB column, no IFRS column.

Securities MTM (MKT_FA = 1.29×)

Weighted average of 9 auditor Anhang disclosures. Range 1.03–1.45×. Paderborn 1.33×, Köln 1.33×, Fulda 1.19–1.45×, Bamberg 1.31×, Würzburg 1.03–1.06×, Aachen 1.06×.

Property Revaluation (MKT_SA = 2.5× blended)

v2.0: Split approach. Sakralgebäude (cathedrals, ~200 at diocesan level) at 0× (cultural heritage). All other diocesan property (offices, residential, commercial) at ~2.78× → blended ~2.5×. Source: Bamberg Verkehrswert, cost-basis dioceses 2.0–3.5× uplift. Does NOT include Layer 7 land.

Pension IAS 19 → Buyout (1.3× then +15–35%)

HGB discount ~1.9%. IAS 19 uses iBoxx AA curve (~3.2%). Buyout adds 15–35% over IAS 19 DBO (Willis Towers Watson benchmarks). Three layers: KZVK deficit €3.5–4.5B, Emeritenanstalt €3.0–4.0B under IAS 19, on-BS provisions remeasured.

Land Valuation (v2.0: ~186K ha, excl. church footprint)

v2.0: 4,600 ha church footprint (16,700 churches × ~2,500m² avg plot) zeroed — cultural heritage, unmarketable. Remaining: 112.5K ha agricultural @ €30K/ha, 75K ha forest @ €14K/ha, 12.9K ha urban @ €150/m². Erbbaurecht unchanged: DCF at 15–20× cap rate. Total Layer 7 mid: €30.5B (was €33.8B).

Parish Triangulation (v2.0: churches at €0)

v2.0: 16,700 church buildings = €0 (cultural heritage). Non-church buildings (rectories, halls, Kitas, rental) valued at full 2026 market — no institutional discounts. Cross-checked: Cologne parishes (already €0 for churches), Augsburg KPV (church component stripped), Speyer (stripped). Consensus: €10.5B non-church property + €3.9B financial = €14.4B ±€3.0B.

Intercompany Eliminations

€18B diocesan deposits at church banks (asset for diocese, liability for bank). €35.9B KZVK overlap (AuM double-counted with diocesan pension provisions). €2–5B other interco (housing subsidiary equity, cross-entity receivables). Total: €56–59B eliminated.

Environmental / Heritage (New Line)

24,000+ listed buildings × conservation mandates. Asbestos/remediation liabilities. Deferred maintenance backlog. Range €3–8B. No HGB equivalent — this liability does not appear in any published account.
