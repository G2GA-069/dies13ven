Methodology Notes

L1 Diocesan KdöR: 27 balance sheets extracted from audited Jahresabschlüsse. €34.8B total HGB Bilanzsumme (27/27 fully extracted, 100% HIGH confidence). Top 3: Paderborn €4.9B, Köln €4.4B, München €4.0B. EK €20.5B (59.8%), Rückstellungen €10.7B, Verbindlichkeiten €2.5B. Hidden reserves €5.3B identified. True MV ~€54B after mark-to-market.

L2 Shadow KdöR: 27/27 dioceses now covered. €5.9B known floor (up from €4.96B). Erfurt surprise at €480M. Paderborn €2.7B still dominates. Berlin/Dresden-Meißen confirmed: no separate Stuhl assets. Regensburg €242M (audited). Est. €7–16B total.

L3 Church Banks: CORRECTED. Liga €6.4B (prior €14.9B was managed volume, not BS), DKM €5.1B (up from €3.2B), Pax+BKC merged Jan 2025 = €8.7B, BIB Essen €4.5B (was missing). Total €24.7B. Intercompany deposits ~€13B (not €18B).

L4 KZVK Pension: €35.9B total assets (2024, up from €34.1B). Funding ratio 89.1% (HGB). HIDDEN: Ausgleichsposten €6.9B deferred liability + IAS 19 gap €2-3B = total economic deficit €9-10B. 1.5M members, 244K pensioners, 5,000 employers.

L5 Housing GmbH: 14 entities identified via KSD member registry. ~94,000 units. ASW (6 dioceses) = €1B BS + €8B AUM subsidiary. Joseph-Stiftung Bamberg €500M, Siedlungswerk R-S €400-600M. Confirmed BS €2.9B, estimated MV €10–20B.

L6 Parishes: ~10,000 Kirchengemeinden with own legal personality. No consolidated data exists. Est. €14.7B based on extrapolation from disclosed diocese-level parish asset data.

L7 Land & Erbbaurecht: 257,290 BORIS zones queried across 16 Bundesländer. Weighted mean €176/m². Church owns est. 500K–825K hectares. €30–42B at BORIS rates.

Key Findings

1. Paderborn dominance: Erzbischöflicher Stuhl alone holds €2.7B — 55% of all known L2 assets. Seven foundation structure obscures true concentration.

2. Bundesanzeiger blind spot: KdöR bodies (Bischöfliche Stühle, Domkapitel) have ZERO filing obligation. Only 1 GmbH shell (EBV Berlin, EK €30M) files regularly. The Bundesanzeiger is structurally useless for L2 discovery.

3. Book vs Market: Augsburg's Stuhl at €675M book could be €900M+ MV. Passau jumped from €190M (2013) to €416M (2019) — 119% growth in 6 years, largely mark-to-market.

4. East German surprise: Erfurt Stuhl = €480M (4th largest in Germany). Berlin/Dresden-Meißen have NO separate Stuhl assets. Görlitz €56M (2014). The assumption that East German dioceses are uniformly small is wrong.

5. Domkapitel are tiny: Only 3 published. Osnabrück €12M, Limburg €5.1M, Augsburg €3.7M. Not material to the aggregate.

6. L5 Housing is massive: Prior €6-10B estimate was too low. ASW alone has €1B BS + €8B AUM. 14 entities, ~94K units. The church is one of Germany's largest residential landlords.

7. L7 is the true dark asset: €30–42B in land, carried at historical cost or zero on parish balance sheets. BORIS data proves the value exists. Zero consolidated disclosure anywhere.
